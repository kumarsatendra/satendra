About Me

I am a Ph.D. candidate in Department of Computer Science and Engineering, at Indian Institute of Technology Kharagpur. I am working under the supervision of Prof. Sudip Misra. I obtained my master's degree, M.Tech. (Gold Medalist), in Computer Technology from National Institute of Technology, Raipur in 2014 under the supervision of Prof. Anamika Yadav. I have received my bachelor's degree, B. Tech (Honors), in Computer Science and Engineering from Uttar Pradesh Technical University, Lucknow in the year 2012. Prior to joining Ph.D, I have served as a faculty in the Department of Information Technology at Bengal College of Engineering and Technology, Durgapur.
